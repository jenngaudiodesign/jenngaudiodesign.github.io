A little about me. 

"I strive to save the world from bad design while creating lasting experiences."

Jenn Gaudio is a web designer, photographer, audiophile, and illustrator in the digital age. Trained as a Graphic Designer with focuses in web and UX development, she performs at the fulcrum of beauty and graphic arts to craft memorable experiences both online and in the real world. Her most recent personal work consists of CMYK vector illustrations invoking feelings of Andy Warhols “Marilyn” and pop art. ” Expected graduation in the spring of 2019.
